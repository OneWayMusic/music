Why Us?

1

Music in Ranchi - A Melodic Fusiony

Ranchi, Jharkhand's capital, resonates with diverse music, blending tribal traditions and modern influences. "One Way Music" showcases local talents and offers a rich array of genres, from tribal to folk and hip hop tribal fusion.

2

Tribal Music - Timeless Rhythms

Ranchi's tribal communities cherish ancient musical traditions, deeply rooted in nature and folklore. "One Way Music" preserves the enchanting authenticity of these melodies, connecting generations with nagpuri songs.

3

Pop Tribal Fusion - Uniting Styles

Witness the rise of Pop tribal songs, a unique fusion of contemporary and traditional elements. "One Way Music" empowers artists to share stories of struggle and identity, captivating local and global audiences.

4

Rediscovering Folk Songs - Melodic Memories

Folk songs hold a special place in Ranchi's heart, and "One Way Music" revives classics with modern flair. Experience the charm of "Nadi Ker Teere," "Dcm Usha Sabun," and the cover of "Bombay X Pilabe Pilabe." Each tune encapsulates life's essence in melodious ways.

Who We Are?

What we do?

Music brings people together. It allows us to experience the same emotions. People everywhere are the same in heart and spirit. No matter what language we speak, what color we are, the form of our politics, or the expression of our love and faith, music proves that we are all connected.

Why choose us?

We believe that true change starts from within. To bring peace to the world, we must first cultivate peace within ourselves. To spread love, we must embody love. Our true value lies not in material possessions but in the qualities that define us.

Where we are?

We strive for excellence, continuously pushing beyond our previous limits. Like the eagle soaring in the sky, we embrace our full potential, never forgetting the journey that has brought us here. Our location is wherever we can make a difference.
